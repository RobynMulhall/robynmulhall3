Why students chose these courses



At ITB we offer a variety of courses in all areas. Whether you desire a full time course, a part time course or a night course we've got the courses for you. Our course areas include:


  • Apprenticeship and Trades
  • Business
  • Community and Youth Development
  • Computing
  • Creative Digital Media
  • Early Childhood Care & Education
  • Engineering
  • Horticulture
  • Languages
  • Sports Management and Coaching

So why do students choose these courses? I conducted a survey and interviewed a large number of students. They were presented with the question of 'Why did you choose your course?'. From this research it was discovered that students had different reasons for choosing their courses.


Student responses have been listed below:

Horticulture - "I've an interest in Horticulture and the outdoors"

Computing in Information and Technology - "I enjoy programming, video games and all things related to technology"


Community and Youth Development - "I want to empower young people and social change"


Creative Digital Media - "It has so many unique routes, there's many roads to oppertunities"


Applied Studies in Social Care - "I wanted to work with people that have different backgrounds to my own"


Computer Systems - "I wanted to do the Digital Forensics and Cyber studies
